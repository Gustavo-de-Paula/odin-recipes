Banana Smoothie

Description

Healthy Banana Smoothie, made with just 4 ingredients. This easy smoothie blends up deliciously thick and creamy. It's full of rich banana flavor and is a good source of potassium.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sliced banana
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender. Blend until smooth, adding more milk if needed to reach desired consistency.
  2. Serve immediately.
